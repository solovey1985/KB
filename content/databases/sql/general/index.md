## Study Flow

1. Learn query shape and row filtering in [basic](sql-basic.interview.md).
2. Move to grouping, subqueries, constraints, and transactions in [intermediate](sql-intermediate.interview.md).
3. Use [advanced](sql-advanced.interview.md) for window functions, recursive CTEs, and richer SQL patterns.
4. Finish with [optimization](sql-optimization.interview.md) and the optimization concept map.
