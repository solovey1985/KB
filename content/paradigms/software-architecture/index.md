2. Move to [Design Patterns and Principles](design-patterns-principles/index.md) and [Scalability and Performance Considerations](scalability-performance/index.md) to connect structure to runtime behavior.
3. Continue with [Cloud Computing and DevOps](cloud-devops/index.md) and [Data Management and Integration](data-management-integration/index.md) to cover delivery and information flow.
4. Add [Reliability, Maintenance, and Evolution](reliability-maintenance-evolution/index.md) and [Communication and Networking](communication-networking/index.md) to prepare for real-world trade-offs.
5. Finish with evaluation, emerging trends, and team communication topics.
